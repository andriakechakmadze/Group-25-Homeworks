About Us

Welcome to Goal Oriented Academy (GOA), your premier online academy based in Georgia, dedicated to mastering programming skills. Founded and directed by Nika Keshelava, GOA serves a thriving community of 1300-1400 students across Georgia.

  • At GOA, our team includes experienced lecturers such as Mr. Luka Tskvaradze, who bring industry expertise to our courses. Classes are conducted on Discord, providing an interactive learning environment where students can collaborate and learn from each other.

  • We encourage leadership development within our student body. Work hard and aim to become the leader of your squad, as leadership at GOA includes opportunities for financial compensation, recognizing your commitment and skills.

  • Join us at GOA and embark on a journey to become a proficient programmer. Let's shape the future of technology together in Georgia!
